Sean Wayne Sebastian

December 19, 1988 - March 15, 2023

Funeral Video

Obituary

Sean Wayne Sebastian, age 34 of Morgantown, KY passed away at his residence on the 15th day of March 2023. Sean was born on Monday, the 19th day of December 1988 in Leitchfield, KY. He was the son of Leslie and Dirk Adkins of Morgantown, KY. Sean is survived by his parents Leslie and Dirk Adkins; two children Sean and Sadie Sebastian; three brothers Chris Sebastian (Miranda), Wyatt and Ayden Adkins; one sister Kayley McMullan (Jordan); grandmother Kathy Twitty; grandfathers Elton Adkins and Ronnie Baxter and several aunts, uncles and cousins. He is preceded in death by his aunt Ashley Phelps and grandmother Janice Adkins. Sean served in the United States Marine Corps starting in 2011. He was a lover of all things nerdy- collecting pokemon cards, having lightsaber battles, and playing video games all day and night. He was saved and baptized at Barnett's Lick Baptist Church. He loved God and spoke about Him often. He loved singing in the shower, traveling to new destinations, hanging out with his little brothers and sister, and spending time with his parents and grandmother. His children, Sean and Sadie, were his whole world. He loved to spoil them any chance he could get and he loved them more than anything. We love you so much Sean, We can't wait to see you again and give you the biggest hug. Give Ashley lots of hugs for us. "Spartans never die, they're just missing in action" - Halo 3

Funeral services will be held at the Jones Funeral Chapel on Tuesday, March 21, 2023 at 1:00pm with Bro. Chad Bewley officiating. Burial will be in the Hill Haven Memory Garden. Visitation will be held on Monday, March 20, 2023 from 5:00pm - 8:00pm and Tuesday 8:00 am until funeral time at 1:00pm at the Jones Funeral Chapel. Please share any photos, memories, condolences or light a candle in memory of Sean at www.jonesfuneralchapel.com. Jones Funeral Chapel is honored to be serving the family of Sean Wayne Sebastin.
